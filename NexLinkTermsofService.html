NexLink is a mobile application designed for use with Anser inkjet printers in industrial environments. The App provides:

  • File synchronization with Google Drive for centralized file management across factory operations.

3. Google Drive Authorization

3.1 Authorization

To use the file synchronization feature, you must sign in with a Google Account and authorize NexLink to access your Google Drive. This authorization is handled through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol.

3.2 Why Full Drive Access Is Required

In a typical industrial workflow, print message files and related resources are prepared and uploaded to Google Drive by factory administrators using other applications, web browsers, or desktop computers — not by NexLink itself. NexLink then downloads these pre-uploaded files from your Drive to the local device for use with Anser inkjet printers.

Because these files are created by external applications, a narrower permission scope (which would only allow access to files created by NexLink itself) is insufficient. Full Google Drive access is therefore required to enable NexLink to read and download files uploaded by other sources.

3.3 Revoking Authorization

You may revoke NexLink's access to your Google Drive at any time by:

  1. Signing out within the App, or
  2. Visiting your Google Account Permissions page and removing NexLink.

After revocation, NexLink will no longer access your Google Drive until you re-authorize.
